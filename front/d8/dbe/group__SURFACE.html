This function fold the surface around the axis described by dir (direction) and axis (a point on the axis) with input angle. Only points on the input side of the axis will be rotated. The function does its job of the surface is flat and axis is a line on the surface.

Parameters
surfinout Input of the surface to be folded.
dirin direction of the folding axis.
axisin a point on the folding axis.
anglein angle of folding.
sidein indicating point on the side to be rotated (folded).
firstin if true, reset all the point of the surface.

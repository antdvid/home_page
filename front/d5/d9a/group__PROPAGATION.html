void FT_Propagate ( Front *  front)

Propagate the Front for one time step. The process includes advancing the front forward by one step to new positions, redistributing new interface mesh and resolving physical and topological bifurcations. The interface in the front is replaced by a new and propagated one and the old one is freed.
